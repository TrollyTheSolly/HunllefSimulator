Simulator Information:

This tool simulates combat encounters with the Corrupted Hunllef. Adjust your stats, gear, prayer levels, and the number of simulations to run. More simulations will be more accurate, but may take longer to run.

Points to be aware of:
1. This tool simulates Corrupted Hunllef only.
2. The tool assumes perfect play, it takes no damage from floor tiles or tornados. Look at remaining food to see your margin of error.
3. Armour is assumed to be all of the same tier.
4. The Crystal Staff and Bow are assumed to be of perfected quality.
5. Melee DPS is not implemented, if there is interest I can look at adding it.
